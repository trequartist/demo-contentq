ContentQ Product Context Kit
What It Does
Closed-loop content system: Diagnose AI visibility ‚Üí Generate strategy ‚Üí Execute with AI agents ‚Üí Track results ‚Üí Improve
Core Product Vocabulary
AI Authority Score (0-100): Blended visibility across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity
 Playbook: 90-day strategic content plan with recommended "plays" (proven strategies)
 Play: Pre-built content strategy (e.g., "Category Creator", "Trojan Horse")
 Multi-Agent System: Researcher + Strategist + Copywriter working together
 Organizational Memory: Persistent context that learns voice/preferences over time
 AEO/GEO: AI Engine Optimization / Generative Engine Optimization (like SEO, but for LLMs)

Data Model Basics
AI Visibility Score Components
Individual LLM Scores (0-100 each): ChatGPT Browse, ChatGPT No-Browse, Claude, Perplexity
Blended Score Formula: Weighted average based on market share
ChatGPT Browse: 40%
ChatGPT No-Browse: 20%
Claude: 25%
Perplexity: 15%
Update Frequency: Weekly automated testing
Impact Levels (throughout product)
üü¢ High Impact: +4-6 points expected, strong LLM citation patterns
üü° Medium Impact: +2-3 points expected, moderate citation probability
üî¥ Low Impact: +0-1 points, strategic but not AI-focused
‚õî Negative Impact: Could hurt score (promotional tone, no sources, etc.)
Content Attributes
Funnel Stage: TOFU/MOFU/BOFU
AI Impact: High/Medium/Low/Negative
Primary LLM Fit: Which LLM most likely to cite this
Topic Cluster: How it connects to existing authority
Agent Behaviors
Researcher Agent: Finds competitive intel, trending topics, data points
 Strategist Agent: Generates angles, creates briefs (SEO + AEO)
 Copywriter Agent: Drafts content matching voice + optimization guidelines
Memory System: Each interaction stores context in vector DB, retrieves relevant past content/feedback
Copy Tone Guidelines
Diagnostic/Score: Clinical, factual ("Your score is 67/100")
 Strategy/Playbook: Consultative, strategic ("This play addresses your gap in...")
 Calendar: Organized, clear ("Week 1: Foundation topics")
 Creation: Collaborative, educational ("Let's find your angle...")
 Tracking: Motivational, progress-oriented ("Up 4 points this week!")
Always: Explain "why" behind recommendations, show expected impact, make it actionable
Technical Constraints
Weekly LLM testing (not real-time)
Impact predictions based on historical patterns
Memory improves over time (requires multiple interactions)
Content optimization is guidance, not guarantees

üü° Yellow: Medium impact, caution, optimization available
üî¥ Red: Low impact, gap, behind competitors

Copy Principles
Always explain why: Never show score without context
Make it actionable: "Add data" not just "Low impact"
Show expected impact: "+3-5 points" not just "will help"
Positive framing: "Opportunities" not "Problems"
Be specific: Cite posts, queries, competitors by name
Educate: Explain how LLMs work, why structure matters
Tooltip Best Practices
Max 50-75 words
Use bullet points (scannable)
Lead with "why this matters"
End with expected impact or next action
Include relevant LLM names when applicable
